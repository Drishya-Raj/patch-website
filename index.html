Rent Tracking ia a new way of boosting your credit history. We track your rent payments to your landlord or agent. If you pay on items, we report this to the credit reference agency Experian and they add this information to your credit report.

  • Connect your bank account
  • Pay your rent
  • Patch reports your rent payments to Experian
